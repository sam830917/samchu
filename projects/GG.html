Description

Welcome to Genies and Gems, the spellbinding match-3 puzzle game from Jam City (the makers of Cookie Jam & Panda Pop)!

Thieves have stolen the palace treasure! Help Jenni the genie and Trix the fox as they journey through mystical worlds, recovering lost relics, gold bars, and enchanted keys. Make smart matches to stay on path while avoiding cursed stones and overcoming caged gems. Outfox bandits to keep your treasures safe on your colorful adventure!
