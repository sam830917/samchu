Description

In Panda Pop, an evil baboon has kidnapped & trapped precious panda pups in the jungle. Strategically burst matching bubbles to beat the evil baboon & return the cubs to their worried mother! Panda Pop puts your strategy gaming skills to the test like never before! Download Panda Pop for free at the App Store or Google Play today!

  • Unlock and discover dozens of beautiful worlds.
  • Compete with friends and see how your scores stack up.
  • Fun & easy to learn. Challenging & rewarding to win!
  • Characters, animations, and sound effects you will LOVE.
  • Strategy game for iPhone or Android!
